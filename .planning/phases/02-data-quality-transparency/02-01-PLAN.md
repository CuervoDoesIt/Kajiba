Add QualityMetadata Pydantic model to KajibaRecord and wire quality persistence into the submit/export pipeline. Also ensures user annotations (outcome, pain_points) survive through the export pipeline alongside auto-computed quality.

Purpose: Foundation for all quality transparency -- scores must be persisted before they can be displayed, filtered, or browsed.
Output: QualityMetadata model in schema.py, quality persistence in cli.py submit/export, stored quality reads in history/stats.
